## What changed & why

Closed the 3 CIC mismatches: Credit now POSTs `{cccd, consent_granted}` to cic-svc; fallback returns the **same shape** as the service (`cic_group` / `max_overdue_days` / `has_bad_debt` + `overdue_days` alias); `DeclaredForm` carries `id_number` + `cic_consent`. Mortgage seed uses CCCD `001099000003` (120d overdue) so a live `CIC_SVC_URL` yields bad CIC → `manual_review`.
